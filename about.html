MEET OUR MANAGING MEMBER

Daniel Frankel


I have spent my entire career working in finance, first as a certified public accountant specializing in taxation and then as a foreign exchange trader on the Chicago Mercantile Exchange. On a personal level, I have helped my parents manage their finances as they age. This combination of training and experience has led me to become a Daily Money Manager.

I founded MoneyCare Solutions LLC in response to the growing need for assistance with the increasing complexity of our financial lives. Email, online banking and online bill-paying are wonderful conveniences when used properly. They can help us monitor our personal finances on an up-to-the-minute basis, an important factor in minimizing potential losses from fraud. We can put these and other tools to work for our clients, bringing peace of mind to an area of their lives that can otherwise be a source of stress.

Our service is tailored to your specific needs and is performed in a respectful and confidential manner. I would be happy to meet with you for a complimentary consultation.
