OUR SERVICES

We look at your day to day financial situation and help you take steps to ensure that you feel in control. Together we will determine which of our services are best suited for your specific situation. These include the following:

  • Bill Payment: We will review your mail with you, making sure that all bills received are accurate and paid on time. Regular bills such as utilities can be put on automatic payment.

  • Credit cards: We will review your credit card statements with you to ensure that all charges are appropriate, and help you initiate disputes when necessary. This will help reduce the chance of losses due to fraud.

  • Budgeting: We use personal financial software to develop a cash flow budget, helping to bring stability to your daily monetary affairs. You can see where your money is being spent and identify areas that need to be increased or decreased.
  • Checking account: Your checking account statement will be reviewed and reconciled on a regular basis.

  • Tax documents: We will help you gather all of your income tax documents and provide any information that your tax preparers need for your returns. We will also assist in the payment of quarterly estimated taxes.

  • Other services: We will assist you in any other matters related to your day to day finances. Where necessary we will review correspondences with you and help you prepare responses.

  • Family: When appropriate, we will work with family members to bring you the best possible service.
